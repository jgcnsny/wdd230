Philippines Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Name: Philippines Chamber of Commerce

Purpose

Philippines Chamber of Commerce is the voice of Philippine business recognized by government and international institutions. As a proactive catalyst of development, PCC promotes and supports the drive for globally competitive Philippine enterprises in partnership with government, local chambers, and other business organizations.

The main responsibility is to provide focused advocacy for business growth and sustainable development by providing business services for the advancement of grassroots entrepreneurship, chamber development, international trade relations, business innovation and excellence, and operating efficiency. These will be achieved through a professional organization working in close cooperation with various stakeholders in public and private sectors.

Scenarios

  • What resources does the Chamber offer for startups and small businesses?
  • Are there mentorship programs or workshops available for new entrepreneurs?
  • Are there any incentives or programs available for businesses looking to relocate or expand?
